PneuScan – AI-powered pneumonia scanner

PneuScan is an advanced AI-driven application designed to assist in diagnosing pneumonia from chest X-ray images. By leveraging deep learning, this tool accurately identifies signs of pneumonia, helping users detect potential lung infections quickly and efficiently. Whether used by medical professionals or individuals seeking an initial assessment, this project enhances healthcare accessibility through technology.

The application allows users to upload a chest X-ray, which is then processed using a trained neural network model. The AI analyzes the image, providing a confidence-based prediction on whether pneumonia is present. By normalizing and resizing the image for optimal model performance, the system ensures high detection accuracy. The interface, built using Streamlit, offers a seamless experience, making AI-powered diagnostics accessible to all.

PneuScan is more than just a tool—it’s an innovation in medical imaging. By enabling rapid, AI-based assessments, it bridges the gap between technology and healthcare, assisting in early detection and timely intervention.

With Pneumonia Detection, users gain an easy-to-use, reliable method for pneumonia screening. The AI model streamlines the diagnostic process, reducing uncertainty and enabling proactive health decisions. By integrating deep learning with medical imaging, this project contributes to the future of AI-assisted healthcare, improving diagnostic accuracy and accessibility.

Pneumonia Detection represents the future of AI-assisted medical diagnostics, offering a cost-effective and scalable solution for pneumonia screening. By reducing reliance on traditional diagnostic methods, which can be time-consuming and resource-intensive, this AI-powered tool empowers individuals and healthcare professionals to make quicker, more informed decisions.
